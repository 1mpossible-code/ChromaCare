Information

Welcome to ChromaCare! We're here to help make the web more inclusive for colorblind individuals by providing ratings and suggestions to improve color accessibility on websites. Our tool evaluates the color contrast and brightness of your website's palette and provides actionable recommendations to make it more accessible for all users.

How It Works

To get started, simply enter the URL of the website you want to check. Our tool will analyze the website's color palette and provide a rating for its accessibility.
